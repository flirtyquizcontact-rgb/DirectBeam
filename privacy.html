Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how DirectBeam: File Transfer accesses, processes and protects information when you use the Android application.

Effective date: June 19, 2026 · Last updated: June 19, 2026

1. Scope and contact

This policy applies to DirectBeam: File Transfer, package name com.quizfans.directbeam (“DirectBeam”, “the app”, “we”, “us”).

Privacy contact: flirtyquiz.contact@gmail.com.

2. No DirectBeam account

DirectBeam does not require you to create or sign in to a DirectBeam account. We do not operate a DirectBeam cloud account database for the app.

3. Files selected for transfer

DirectBeam accesses only files, folders or media that you select or authorize through Android permissions and system pickers. These items are processed to provide the transfer functions you initiate.

  • Android-to-Android: selected files and associated transfer metadata are sent directly to the receiver you approve. Secure Transport V6 uses authenticated AES-256-GCM encryption and integrity verification during supported Android-to-Android sessions.
  • PC Connect: selected or authorized files are made available through a temporary authenticated HTTP session on your local network. PC Connect is not end-to-end encrypted and should be used only on a trusted private network.
  • No DirectBeam cloud storage: DirectBeam does not upload your selected files to a server operated by us.

Depending on the file and Android access granted, metadata may include file name, size, MIME type, modification time, relative path and metadata already embedded in the file, such as media or EXIF information. DirectBeam transfers file bytes without intentionally removing embedded metadata.

4. Device discovery and local connectivity

To discover and connect compatible devices, DirectBeam may use Wi-Fi Direct, local-network communication, Nearby Connections, Bluetooth-related discovery capabilities and Android location-related permissions where required by the operating system. These capabilities are used to locate devices, establish transfers and diagnose connection availability.

DirectBeam does not use these permissions to build a location history. Android or Google Play services may require certain permissions or process technical signals to provide nearby-device discovery.

5. Information stored locally

The app may store settings, transfer history, diagnostics, saved folder permissions, purchase status and related operational records on your device. This local information remains under your device storage and can generally be removed from inside the app, by clearing the app’s storage, or by uninstalling the app.

6. Advertising and consent

The free version may use Google Mobile Ads. The app also uses Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request and manage consent where required.

Depending on your region, consent choices, device settings and Google’s configuration, Google’s advertising services may process information such as device or advertising identifiers, IP address, general device information, app interactions, ad interactions and diagnostics for advertising delivery, fraud prevention, security, measurement and related purposes.

Google acts under its own terms and privacy practices. You may manage available consent choices in the app and through your device or Google settings.

7. Google Play Billing

If you purchase DirectBeam Pro, the transaction is processed by Google Play. We do not receive your full payment-card details. The app may receive purchase status, product identifier, transaction token and related entitlement information needed to unlock, restore and validate the purchase.

8. Google Play services used by the app

DirectBeam includes Google Play services for Nearby Connections, QR/code scanning, advertising consent, advertising and billing. These providers may process technical data necessary to deliver their services, subject to their own policies.

The current app build does not include Firebase Analytics or Firebase Crashlytics.

9. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal files or personal information. Information may be processed or shared only in the following limited contexts:

  • with the receiving device or computer you intentionally connect to;
  • with Google service providers used for ads, consent, billing, code scanning and nearby connectivity;
  • when required to comply with applicable law, protect users, prevent fraud or defend legal rights.

10. Security

We use technical safeguards appropriate to the app’s functions, including authenticated sessions, integrity checks and encryption for supported Android-to-Android Secure Transport V6 transfers. No method of electronic transmission or storage is completely risk-free.

Network safety: use PC Connect only on a trusted private network. Do not expose the PC Connect address or port to the public internet.

11. Retention and deletion

  • Transferred files: retained on the devices chosen by the user until deleted by the device owner.
  • Local app records: retained on the device until deleted in the app, cleared through Android settings or removed by uninstalling the app.
  • Purchase records: retained by Google Play according to Google’s legal and operational requirements.
  • Advertising and consent data: retained by Google or its service providers according to their applicable policies and settings.

DirectBeam has no user-account database to delete. Details and request instructions are available on the Data Deletion and Local Data Controls page.

12. Children

DirectBeam is a general utility and is not designed to collect personal information from children. Users must comply with the age and supervision requirements applicable in their country and under Google Play rules.

13. International processing

Third-party service providers may process technical information in countries other than your own. Their processing is governed by their respective privacy terms and applicable safeguards.

14. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when app functions, service providers or legal requirements change. The updated version will be published on this page with a revised date.

15. Contact

Questions or privacy requests: flirtyquiz.contact@gmail.com.
